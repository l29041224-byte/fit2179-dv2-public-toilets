When Nature Calls

Public toilet accessibility in Melbourne

1. Where are the toilets?

This map shows the location of public toilets recorded in the City of Melbourne dataset. The points are zoomed in to make the CBD and nearby areas easier to read.

2. What facilities are provided?

This chart counts only toilets where each facility is recorded as available. It makes the facility gaps easier to see than a stacked total chart.

3. Which features are common?

This chart shows the percentage split between available, unavailable and unknown records. Baby-change facilities are the clearest gap.

4. Is access keeping up with population?

This chart uses the current public toilet count and ABS population estimates for Melbourne LGA. It does not show historical toilet construction. Instead, it shows how population growth can increase pressure on the existing toilet network.

Data sources: City of Melbourne Public Toilets dataset; ABS Data by Region, Population and people, LGA, 2011–2024. Author: Rick Liu. Date: 2026.
